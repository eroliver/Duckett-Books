This is a photo using only HTML and I'm sure you don't often see images online like this anymore. Notably, this picture has a fixed size of 200 pixels by 200 pixels, and may seem large on your phone, but small on your computer.
You probably never encounter images like this anymore because most people browse online on their phones and even between phones there is quite a range of sizes. It would be much less user friendly if you always had to deal with images
of varying sizes. We will revisit this later when we use CSS to shape images dynamically based on our display size.

HTML Tables
